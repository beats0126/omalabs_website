My Journey

The Path to Mastery

With a background in Game Development (B.Sc. Hons), my journey began with a fascination for how systems interact. Over the last 7+ years, that curiosity evolved into a career leading multidisciplinary teams.

My passion for Hardware Engineering allows me to bridge the gap between the digital and physical worlds, from PCB design to IoT implementation. Today, I lead as a Tech Lead, mentoring the next generation of developers while staying hands-on with the latest in AI automation and LLM integration.
